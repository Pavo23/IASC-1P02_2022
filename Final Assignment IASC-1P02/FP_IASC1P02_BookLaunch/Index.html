About the Book

Catherine is no superwoman. She has seen her fair share of face plants. The ones that are painful and embarrassing. Maybe you can relate.
Her story is a lot like yours... She is a pretty simple person in a not so simple world. Journeying through life she has encountered many detours and challenges that were both heart breaking and life changing. Stepping through life with all it's choices and changes gave her a vision that has become her mission and the foundation to write Face Plant.

In this book you will learn how to:

  • find the bridge through hardship and heartache to hope and healing
  • create pathways to overcome lies and find freedom
  • get direction and wisdom to navigate life and become the change agents we are created to be in this broken world

When you have experienced the pain, hurt and self-doubt that comes with life's "face plants", get ready for an infusion of hope. As a certified life coach and years of experience in trauma healing, Catherine offers in this book practical steps of how to let go of doubts, fears and lies. She outlines how to find freedom from living with blindspots and wounds, pursue your calling, transition through challenges with wisdom and grace, and experience strength and security, so you can live life boldly with joy and intention. Don't stay down. This book will tell you how to get up again!

About the Author

Catherine Gillard is a certified life coach and teacher with over 30 years in education and in ministry to women and families.

Hello, I am Catherine!

I love hiking and secret pathways. I love falling leaves and glittery snow. I love kids and giggles, friendships and laughter, and my little family of 5 growing men and now one daughter in law. But most of all I have to say I love "aha moments". For me NOTHING beats the way someone big or small looks when they learn or unlearn and understand something new in a way they didn't see it before. That's been a guiding gift through my lifetime: as a non stop learner/student of life, a public school teacher, a parent, a spouse, a children & youth pastor, a women's group leader, a trauma healing training facilitator and a certified life coach. Nothing beats this for me... this is my greatest life gift.
As a professional ICF certified christian life coach I bring careful listening, challenging questions and proven foundational strategies to women, families and couples like you. My heart's call is to bring clarity and wisdom for you to find your truth to reformulate and succeed in your next step in life in whatever challenge you may face. At any stage of life we can find unforeseen grief, loss, and change. Sometimes a "pile up" of circumstances have gotten us to a place where we feel stuck and can't see the next step. My mission is to help you process your circumstances in a healing path that leads to growth, clarity and transformation. From surviving to thriving. My book "Face Plant" is an extension of this vision.
